City Overview

Bangalore, also called Bengaluru, is the capital of Karnataka, India. It is known for its pleasant weather all year round. The city is often called the "Silicon Valley of India" because it is a major hub for IT companies.


Instead of just being an IT hub, Bangalore is known for its vibrant culture, delicious food, and beautiful gardens, making it a lively place to live and work.


From historical landmarks to modern infrastructure, this city offers a perfect blend of tradition and innovation.




Quick Facts

Language: Kannada
Population: 12.3 million
State: Karnataka
Famous: IT Industry, Startup
Climate: Moderate
